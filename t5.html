This program fetures an text editor, a method for inserting and manipulating graphic images, and a slide-show system to display the content.

Pre-requisites

  • LibreOffice
  • Hvae a functional Mac

Steps

  1. Go to your applications and Open LibreOffice
  2. Click on "Impress" icon (figure 14)
  3. Input the Information Clicking on the slides(figure 15)
  4. Click icons on the Formatting tool bar to change your text look

    The formatting tool bar will change depending on what you want to edit (slide, text, ect.)

  5. Click on the standard tool bar to perfom additional commands

    Save, Print, Paste, and opnem new document

  6. Select the Floppy disk
  7. Choose file name and location
  8. Click "Save"
  9. Close the program
