Use this program as a spell checker, a thesaurus, a dictionary, and txt editing to create a Txt Documents.

Context

Only use LibreOffice Writer when you need to write a document.

Pre-requisites

  • Download LibreOffice suite
  • Have a funtional Mac

Steps

  1. Go to your applications and Open LibreOffice
  2. Click on "Writer" icon (figure 8)
  3. Input the Information Clicking on the paper(figure 9)
  4. Click icons on the Formatting tool bar to change your text look

    Like Font size, Font style, bullets, and highlight text

  5. Click on the standard tool bar to perfom additional commands

    Save, Print, Paste, and opnem new document

  6. Select the Floppy disk
  7. Choose file name and location
  8. Click "Save"
  9. Close the program
